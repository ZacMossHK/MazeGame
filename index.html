In Maze Game the aim where the aim is to reach your goal without falling in any holes!


This is your player.


This is your goal.


This is the path you have taken so far.


This is a hole. If you touch one the game is over!


To play, press one of the four directional buttons to move: Up, Down, Left, and Right, or one of the four arrow keys.


To start again, press Reset.


Press Memory to play a pre-made map, or Random to have one randomly generated for you!


If you are feeling creative, press Custom to enter your own settings.


Loading times will be longer if you choose Random or Custom.


Have fun!
